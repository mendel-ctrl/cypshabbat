Why CYP Shabbat

Because Jewish life is better when we experience it together.

CYP Shabbat is designed for Jewish young adults looking for real community — not awkward networking, not a formal service, and not just another night out. It is an opportunity to meet, celebrate, recharge, and begin Shabbat surrounded by people at a similar stage of life.
